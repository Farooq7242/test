Mangoes from Pakistan

Mango Varieties from Pakistan

Alphonso

Originated in the Konkan region of India, the Alphonso mango is known for its sweet, juicy flesh and rich, creamy texture. It is considered one of the finest mangoes in the world.

Popular brands using Alphonso mangoes:
- Fruit Bazaar
- Mango King
- Sweet and Juicy

Chaunsa

The Chaunsa mango is a sweet and juicy variety, grown in the Northern regions of Pakistan. It has a dense flesh and a rich, sweet flavor that makes it a popular choice for mango lovers.

Popular brands using Chaunsa mangoes:
- Mango Delight
- Sweet Treats
- Fresh Delight

Dusheri

The Dusheri mango is a sweet and fragrant variety, grown in the Northern regions of Pakistan. It has a juicy flesh and a unique, sweet flavor that makes it a popular choice for mango lovers.

Popular brands using Dusheri mangoes:
- Mango Magic
- Sweet Sensation
- Juicy Delight
